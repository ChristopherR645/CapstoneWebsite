Game Jams

A game jame is an event where teams try to make a video game from scratch, usually limited to a few days. The two games I created have been in collaboration with my cousin and developed for the 2020 & 2021 GMTK game jam. Both games were developed in Unity using C#.

Cassette

Theme: Out of control
The first game we created together and first time using Unity. The gameplay revolves around recording your movements without visual feedback then playing them back allowing you to watch the character try and navigate the levels.

Swinging into it

Theme: Joined Together
Our second attempt at the game jam. Equipped with more experience and another member we went for a physics based swining platformer. In this game you swing platform to platform switching between which side you control to reach the end goal.
